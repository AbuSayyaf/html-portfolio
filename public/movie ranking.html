The Best Movies According to Abu Bakr

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shogun

This is my favorite historical movie. The movie is based in real life on how Edo era in Japan resisted Catholic expansionism by the Portuguese and the Spaniards.

Halo

Best sci-fi series film. It is about defending the human race from covenant, an Advanced Alien life form wich conquers and destroy other planets. The humans develop an advanced human fighting machines called Spartans equipped with special armors and have high-tech systems built-in the suits that gives the spartans special abilities.

Jack Reacher

Ex-marine created a private investigation team composed of ex-marine. His previous team in the army now working with him in solving cases and preventing terrorist plots in his country.
